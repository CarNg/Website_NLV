Game Design

I began my love affair with game design during the course of my MSc where I developed and designed a number of digital as well as non-digital games. Learning about game development and game design has been a new and exciting adventure and I am thrilled to be able to share some of these projects with you.

NB: As these projects were a part of college assignments from a game design and games programming POV, original audio and art work weren't always implemented. Therefore, some art and audio work from these projects are from external sources. Information on each project regarding this will be expanded on individually.

Visual Media

Visual Communication has always been something that has fascinated me and I've always wanted to explore it. Luckily, I was able to do that during my undergraduate and postgraduate courses. I was able to delve into the world of visual media, from photography and videography to web design and graphic design.

Between the two courses and a number of personal projects, I've been able to work with and gain skills in a number of softwares and languages. This includes the Adobe Creative Suite and Final Cut, and languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery.
