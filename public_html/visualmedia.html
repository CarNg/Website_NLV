Portrait Photography (2009-2011)
Photography - Series of Portraits

Portraits have always been a strange thing to me. We are surrounded by people but how often to we really look at people. How odd would it be to just sit and stare at someone? To examine them and gauge how they feel just with our sight. Portraits allow us to do that. It's one moment where we can truly look at someone. How the shadow falls on their face, their body posture, their smile, their frown, their movement. It's all there.

A series of portraits taken in and out of the studio, tactfully and candidly.
