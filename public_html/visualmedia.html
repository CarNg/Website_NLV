Night photography and long exposures has always been a side of photography that I loved. There's something about them that, to me, simultaneously freezes and captures the passing of time perfectly. Portrait Photography (2009-2011)
Photography - Series of Portraits

Portraits have always been a strange thing to me. We are surrounded by people but how often to we really look at people. How odd would it be to just sit and stare at someone? To examine them and gauge how they feel just with our sight. Portraits allow us to do that. It's one moment where we can truly look at someone. How the shadow falls, their posture, their smile, their frown. It's all there.

A series of portraits taken in and out of the studio, tactfully and candidly.
